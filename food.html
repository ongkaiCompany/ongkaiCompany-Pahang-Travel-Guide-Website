Mee

Curry Mee

Unlike Penang's version of curry mee, the curry broth in Pahang's version of curry mee is less creamy with more balanced flavours.


Each bowl of curry mee comes with yellow noodles, bihun,and/or koay teow cooked in curry broth topped off with shredded steamed chicken, fish balls, fish cakes, beansprouts, and tofu puffs.

Rice

Nasi Tomato

Nasi Tomato (“Tomato Rice”) is rather one of the popular traditional rice dishes which you can find in Pahang and also in other states of Malaysia.


Nasi Tomato can sometimes be found, as in replacement of plain white rice, in some established restaurants and hotels as part of the Malay cuisines offering both for lunch or dinner.

Nasi kebuli, considered a heritage dish of the Kuala Lipis district, is made of rice cooked with coconut oil and chicken, sometimes substituted with lamb or mutton. It includes raisins, cloves, olives, and lemongrass, which give it an aromatic smell.
