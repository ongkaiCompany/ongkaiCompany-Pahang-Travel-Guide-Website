Objective

lah is a famous suffix we Malaysians (and Singaporeans) use to complement any sentence available in a social conversation. The origins of this slang are basically from the Malay and Chinese languages. The main objective of this website is to promote the tourist industry in Pahang. This website is going to offer everything that you need to travel to Pahang for instance where to get the cheapest accommodation, what to eat and what to play.
